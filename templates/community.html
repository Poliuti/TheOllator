Drop it like it's hot
The Ollator is an application allowing singers or rap / hip hop music fans to quickly get a beat for their music projects or just for fun. The idea of The Ollator is to give the possibility to users to get a beat that resembles his/her taste exploiting our machine learning algorithms with low effort.

The core of our application lies in the community built around it: to ensure for our machine learning algorithms to have the maximum precision it is fundamental the contribution from each and every one of you (bigger database means higher precision)! So by uploading the midi stems of one of your beats, you will contribute to enlarge our family and you will provide a better service for everyone (without mentioning that your name and link to your artist page will be mentioned to our users ;) ).

Upload your midi file containing four tracks:

Drum track, arranging the kick on the C1 note, the snare on D1 and the hi hat on E1.
Bass track
Harmony track
Melody track

Be sure to set the tempo at 120 bpm before exporting the midi file and to name your tracks properly (i.e. name your bass track "bass", harmony "harmony", ...).
